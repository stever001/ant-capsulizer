The Studio

A Fun & Excellence-Driven Environment

My studio provides consistent motivation by offering competitive performances, CD recordings, master classes, recitals, computer theory games, and gentle guidance, reinforced with lots of positive reinforcement.

With private piano lessons, your child will learn to master the techniques that define the makings of an accomplished musician. The Piano Journey Studio is the first step for all students who wish to become successful musical artists while having fun at the same time.

Private Piano Lessons
Let me teach your child to love music through piano

Teaching children to love music through piano appreciation and mastery. It is well known that students with a musical background fair better in school than those without. Students learn to memorize and to perform pieces with technical accuracy and musical expression. The only thing they have to do is show up (and practice at home)!

Studio Policies

After enrolling in The Piano Journey Studio, one month’s tuition deposit is required in addition to the first month’s tuition.

With 30 days notice of quitting, the deposit will serve as the last month’s tuition payment. Tuition is due on or before the first lesson of each month. A late fee of $10 will be charged to the music account if the payment arrives after the first lesson.

Instruction from a Professional Musician

degreed & certified teacher with more than 20 years experience

Maria Mclean is a degreed and certified teacher with more than 20 years of experience. Maria was fortunate enough to have studied with the acclaimed concert pianist, Louis Galanffy, to whom she credits her great love of the piano.

Maria established The Piano Journey Studio with the hope of providing children the opportunity to learn to play the piano in an environment filled with enthusiasm and fun.

CD Recordings

Enrich the learning experience with CD Recordings

The Piano Journey Studio provides more than just training for piano students. Through the use of compact disc recordings, students have the chance to hear themselves performing pieces they have learned. Enrich your home with the sounds of music created by your children!
