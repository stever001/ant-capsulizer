Video Services

Remote Videography
Don’t settle for a Zoom recording. Our videographers provide you with a trial-quality recording of the witness. This means there are no other participants visible. When exhibits are shared, our picture-in-picture video allows a viewing of the exhibit without losing sight of the witness.
Video Synchronization
T&T’s video staff will sync the reporter’s transcript (or segments of it) to the video deposition, as requested.
Capture Evidence
Our videographers can do more than document deposition testimony.  They can come on-site for a variety of purposes including (but not limited to) Independent Medical Evaluations, site inspections, workplace conditions, et cetera.

Let us help you with our in house videographer and mobile conferencing services.
