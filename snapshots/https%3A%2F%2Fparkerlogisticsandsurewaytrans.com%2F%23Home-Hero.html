Sureway Transportation Services

Flatbed Transportation

Make sure your freight arrives at its destination quickly with flatbed transportation services from Parker Logistics LP/ Sureway Transportation. We offer partial and full load services with weights up to 48,000 pounds. Tarps are also available for this service.

Heavy Haul

Parker Logistics LP/ Sureway Transportation can haul heavy loads that are wide and tall. Whatever you need transported, we will get it done for you. Our staff organizes escorts and permits.  We can even handle overseas shipping services for you.

Van Transportation

  • Quick Turnaround
  • Reliable Drivers
  • 48′ to 53′ Trailers
  • Maximum 44,000 to 45,000 Lbs.

Reefer Transportation

We provide 48′ to 53′ trailers to haul grass, produce, or anything that needs to be temperature controlled. These freezers can maintain the temperature you desire from pickup to delivery. We can also provide tracking.

Hotshot Services

When you need one or two pallets quickly, we offer hotshot direct services or partial load services on a truck with other load.
