Top Quality Mills & Accessories

RLA Machine & Tool Sales, Inc. offers a variety of new and used machine tools, accessories, and software packages from a select group of top brand manufacturers. From sales to service, our experience professionals are available to assist with all your tool room needs, included on-site installation and training for all the brands we represent. RLA Machine & Tools, Inc. also has the capability to assist in the repair and rebuilding of your existing equipment. Whether you are looking for machine maintenance or replacement parts, our experienced service and technical support staff is available to help you.

Not only do we sell the Digital Readouts and CNC Retrofit packages, we also provide installation and training of all the BRANDS we sell. We also have a constantly changing inventory of used manual mills, CNC vertical mills, bed mills, along with used manual and CNC lathes.
