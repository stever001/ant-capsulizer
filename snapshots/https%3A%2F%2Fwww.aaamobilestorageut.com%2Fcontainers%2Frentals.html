Rental Containers Cedar City UT

If you rely on a regular storage facility with wooden roofs and concrete walls, you aren’t getting true protection while your belongings are stored. Dust collects from above, rolling door locks can be picked or broken, and you may even find your items becoming damaged from water or humidity.

Don’t take that chance when you have AAA Mobile Storage containers at your disposal. As a unique method of storage, the standard shipping containers we rent come in a variety of sizes.  They also feature lockboxes on the outside that prevent tampering, and a tough steel exterior that will keep just about everything out.

No matter the number of boxes, furniture, inventory, or off-season sports equipment, our storage containers will hold it and protect it for as long as you need. Below are the sizes we offer and hints as to the size you may require.

Standard containers

Our standard containers come with cargo doors that are extremely durable and airtight. These containers will keep pests, dust, and water outside where they belong. Some of our containers have cargo doors at both ends to make loading and unloading even easier, especially for oversized items. Additionally, you can pick between single-door or double-door options to further personalize your storage experience.

Office Containers

Looking for a temporary “building” you can use as an office away from your business? Our office containers are just what you need. They come with a lockable door, climate control inside, and electrical plugs to keep your electronics and appliances running indoors. These mobile offices are great for long-term construction projects, industrial projects, or as a base of operations for any application. While these containers are not airtight like our other storage units, they do a great job keeping the elements outside while you work inside.
