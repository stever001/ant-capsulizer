MEET THE TEAM

proudly accepting your business for 29 years.

Founded in 1992, Kingsford Fasteners was named after a bag of charcoal when Mike David and Brian Caudle were in college. Now almost three decades later, Kingsford Packaging provides nationwide service for wholesale and retail packaging with a commitment to customer service. They have been called the hillbillys of the industry by competitors. Kingsford's partnerships with manufacturers, distributors, and service companies put them at the top of the market with direct-to-you prices that are fair and affordable.
