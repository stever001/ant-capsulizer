Got a party in mind?

Let's partY!

Delsa's offers not only great food, but a LOT of space for you to host your next party or event! In addition to Delsa's House, you can rent our our spacious Patio, Yard, and Party Barn for a cumulative space that comfortably accommodates over 250 people! The yard also has a stage for live music and concert events. Customized catering menus are available with a beer and wine selection.
