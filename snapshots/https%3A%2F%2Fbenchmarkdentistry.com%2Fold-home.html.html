Our Treatments

Comprehensive Dental Care for Every Stage of Life

Benchmark Family Dentistry offers full-scope dental care for children, teens, and adults—from prevention and aesthetics to advanced restorations.
View All Services

Preventative Dentistry

We provide thorough cleanings, exams, sealants, and personalized education to help prevent costly treatments later on.

Restorative Dentistry

From simple fillings to full-mouth rehabilitation, our restorative services are designed to bring back function to damaged or decayed teeth.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Enhance your smile with cosmetic options like professional teeth whitening, veneers, and bonding—all crafted to look and feel natural.

Dental Implants

Replace missing teeth with permanent, natural-looking dental implants that restore bite strength and confidence—for one tooth or a full arch.

Same-Day Crowns

Our advanced CEREC® system lets us create and place custom ceramic crowns in a single visit—no goopy impressions, no second appointment.

Pediatric Dentistry

We welcome kids with a warm approach, focusing on dental education, prevention, and helping the little ones feel safe and confident at every visit.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Helping you feel informed and at ease. Here are the answers to the questions we hear most often from our patients.
What dental services do you offer at Benchmark Dentistry?
We provide comprehensive care for the whole family, including cleanings, exams, fillings, crowns, cosmetic treatments, dental implants, and same-day CEREC® crowns—all in one convenient Boise location.
Do you accept dental insurance or offer financing?
Yes! We accept most major insurance plans and offer flexible financing options through CareCredit. Our team is happy to help you understand your benefits and payment choices.
What should I expect at my first dental visit?
Your first appointment includes a full exam, digital X-rays if needed, and time to discuss your dental goals or concerns. We take a no-pressure, judgment-free approach and tailor care to your needs.
Do you treat children and families?
Absolutely. We welcome patients of all ages, including kids and teens. Our gentle approach and family-friendly environment help young patients feel safe, supported, and at ease during every visit.
