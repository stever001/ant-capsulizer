Welcome to Imagine Programs

Imagine Programs has been providing substance abuse treatment services since 2010.

Imagine Programs takes pride in being a small, locally owned business that offers affordable care to our clients. Imagine Programs maintains the tradition of small business by giving personal attention to clients, families and referral sources to ensure each case gets the time and attention it deserves. When it comes to our counseling, keeping our group sizes small also allows us to give more personal attention to each client, and helps create a safe environment for change.

Our Mission

To provide a calm, peaceful, and supportive environment for our clients to seek their inner strength to find their road to recovery.

Our Goals

Our staff is dedicated to providing quality and up to date treatment services for our clients so they are successful in their treatment.
