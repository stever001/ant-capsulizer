Our Services

we provide the finest products for your pool or spa

Keeping a swimming pool clean and healthy is a huge challenge. Even with modern pool chemicals, reading all those labels can be confusing. Pool chemicals work hard to control bacteria, algae and fungus. If mishandled, however, these same chemicals are incompatible and mixing them can result in hazardous fumes or strong reactions.

That’s why we encourage and promote professional, weekly pool cleaning by TLC Pool Service of Hilton Head Inc.. Let our team of experienced and seasoned professionals handle it all for you, so you won’t have to worry about injuries to your family.

Our Maintenance Depart Offers:

  • Check and balance water chemistry
  • We provide chlorine, acid, D.E., BiCarb and soda ash chemicals
  • Empty pump and skimmer baskets
  • Vacuum pool
  • Clean out automatic pool cleaner bag
  • Backwash, as needed and add D.E.
  • Brush and skim pool

We proudly service Pentair and Eco Select products and equipment!
