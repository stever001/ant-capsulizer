Behind on Taxes or Facing IRS Issues?

If you're overwhelmed by IRS letters, garnishments, or unfiled returns, you're not alone. At LeBeau & Associates, we provide calm, professional guidance to help you understand your options and take the next right step forward.

What if I haven’t filed in years?

We’ll help you gather the necessary info, file back returns, and avoid unnecessary penalties.

Can you help with IRS notices or wage garnishments?

Yes. We’ll review the notice, explain your rights, and help you respond strategically.

Will I owe a lot? What if I can’t pay it all?

We can walk you through payment plan options and help minimize your financial stress.
