Holistic Educational Rehabilitation Center

Healing the Mind, Body, and Spirit

At Holistic Educational Rehabilitation Center (HERC), we offer compassionate and personalized support for individuals facing mental health challenges, substance abuse, and addiction. Our services are open to everyone in need of guidance, healing, and a fresh start.

For those struggling with addiction, unhealthy patterns, broken relationships, or a chaotic lifestyle, we provide a safe and supportive space to rebuild. Our comprehensive programs include:

  • Mental Health Counseling
  • Addiction and Substance Abuse Treatment
  • Spiritual Counseling and Inner Healing
  • Trauma-Informed Therapy
  • Life Skills Training

Our caring, experienced staff is dedicated to helping you develop the tools and mindset needed to overcome addiction and take back control of your life. With over 25 years of success, our licensed counselors are here to walk with you every step of the way.

‍You don’t have to face this alone. Let us help you rejuvenate your life—mentally, emotionally, and spiritually.

HERC Treatment Principles

  • We believe in people and treating each person as an individual through the HOLISTIC approach.
  • We believe that it is our God-given responsibility to respond to the needs of individuals and to EDUCATE by securing all of the resources they need to become sober or drug free individuals.
  • We know from experience that chemical dependency is treatable and RECOVERY can be achieved through a healthy support system and a sound clinical plan.
  • At HERC, we know structure is important in living a productive, fulfilled and purposeful life, therefore we help our clients stay COMMITTED to all task & goals placed before them. One way do we do this is by example in our commitment to them!
