Guaran-T-Clean Joins Forces with Valley Carpet Care

Dear valued Customers:

We are thrilled to share the exciting news of Valley Carpet Care's acquisition of Guaran-T-Clean, a business that has been a cornerstone of quality carpet cleaning services in the Treasure Valley since 1981.

Founded by David Gridley, Guaran-T-Clean has been a family-owned and operated business dedicated to delivering exceptional residential and commercial carpet cleaning. David's commitment to guaranteed quality workmanship, fair pricing, and over 43 years of expertise has set a standard of excellence in the industry.

As Valley Carpet Care, we are honored to carry forward David Gridley's legacy. We extend our deepest gratitude to David and the Guaran-T-Clean team for their dedication.

To the valued customers of Guaran-T-Clean, thank you for entrusting us with your cleaning needs. We are committed to maintaining the same level of professionalism, efficiency, and guaranteed quality that you have come to expect.

Welcome to Valley Carpet Care, where we look forward to serving you with passion and dedication, continuing the tradition of excellence that David Gridley has instilled in the heart of our business.
