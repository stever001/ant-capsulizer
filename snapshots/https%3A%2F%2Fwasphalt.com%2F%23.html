How long does it take to install a new driveway?

The timeframe for installing a new driveway can vary depending on factors such as size, complexity, and weather conditions. However, our team works efficiently to minimize disruption to your property, typically completing most driveway installations within a few days.

How often should I seal coat my pavement?

Seal coating helps protect your asphalt surface from the elements and prolong its lifespan. We recommend seal coating your driveway or parking lot every 2-3 years, or as needed based on wear and tear and exposure to harsh weather conditions.

What is the difference between chip & seal and traditional asphalt paving?

Chip & seal, also known as tar & chip paving, involves applying a layer of hot tar to the base surface, followed by a layer of stone chips. This creates a textured, decorative finish. Traditional asphalt paving, on the other hand, involves laying a smooth layer of hot asphalt mix over a prepared base. Both options offer durability and aesthetic appeal, but chip & seal provides a unique rustic look.

How can I maintain my asphalt surface to prolong its lifespan?

Proper maintenance is key to maximizing the longevity of your asphalt surface. This includes regular sweeping to remove debris, prompt repair of cracks and potholes, and periodic seal coating to protect against moisture and UV damage. Our team can provide guidance on recommended maintenance practices tailored to your specific pavement needs.
