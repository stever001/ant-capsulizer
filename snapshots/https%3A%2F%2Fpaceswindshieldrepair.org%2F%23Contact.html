OUR FRIENDLY & PROFESSIONAL STAFF IS HAPPY TO MEET ALL YOUR WINDSHIELD NEEDS.

Pace’s Chip & Rock Windshield Repair & Replacement is one of the fastest growing glass repair and replacement companies in the Sacramento area. Of utmost importance to each of those service calls is our mission of providing professional services with excellent workmanship and attention to detail–always striving to exceed the expectations of each customer.

We understand how important glass is in protecting people while they’re driving. In fact, your windscreen provides 30% of a car’s structural strength and could help save your life in an accident. It follows that our people, products and systems are equally as important in keeping drivers, and those they care about, safe. It’s at the heart of everything we do.

That’s why when we replace your windscreen; we only use glass from car manufacturers’ suppliers. It’s as good as an original because we are using glass that has undergone the same stringent quality tests as the glass that the car manufacturers use.
