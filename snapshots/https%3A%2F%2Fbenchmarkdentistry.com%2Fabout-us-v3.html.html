Services

Mental Health Services

These services work together to help individuals improve their mental well-being and achieve recovery.

Individual Therapy

Personalized one-on-one sessions with licensed therapists to address your unique mental health needs.

Group Therapy

Supportive group sessions where individuals can share experiences and learn from one another.

Online Counseling

Convenient and confidential virtual therapy sessions that you can attend from the comfort of your home.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Structured therapy aimed at identifying and changing negative thought patterns to improve mental health.

Mindfulness and Meditation

Techniques and practices to help you stay present, reduce stress, and enhance emotional regulation.

Family Counseling

Counseling sessions are designed to improve communication and resolve conflicts within family units.

Medication Support

Helps patients manage their medications through education, and monitoring to ensure proper use and improve health outcomes.

Psychiatric Consultations

Professional consultations to assess, diagnose, and provide medication management for various mental health conditions.

Crisis Intervention

Immediate and effective support for mental health emergencies ensures safety and quick access to care.

Preventative Dentistry

Preventative care is the key to long-term oral health. We focus on protecting your teeth and gums through regular checkups, cleanings, and early intervention—so you can avoid costly procedures down the road.

Exams & Check-ups

Routine exams help us detect issues early and develop a personalized care plan for your smile.

Oral Hygiene

We provide thorough cleanings and guidance to keep your teeth and gums healthy between visits.

Child Dentistry

Our friendly team makes dental visits fun and comfortable for kids of all ages.

Teeth Cleaning

Professional cleanings remove plaque, tartar, and stains to keep your smile fresh and bright.

Gum Disease Treatment

We offer non-surgical gum therapy to treat early signs of periodontal disease and prevent tooth loss.

Dental Sealants

Sealants protect the grooves of molars from decay—especially useful for children and teens.

Tooth Extractions

When a tooth cannot be saved, we perform safe and gentle extractions with clear aftercare.

Sports Mouth Guards

Custom-fit guards help protect teeth from injury during sports and physical activities.


Restorative Dentistry

When damage or decay occurs, our restorative services are designed to bring back function, strength, and comfort—using modern materials that look as good as they feel.

Fillings

We use tooth-colored composite fillings to repair cavities discreetly and durably.

Implants

Dental implants replace missing teeth with natural-looking, long-lasting results.

Crowns

Custom crowns restore the shape, function, and appearance of damaged or weakened teeth.

CEREC®

Using CEREC® technology, we design and place your crown in a single visit—no temporaries required.

Bridges

Dental bridges fill the space of missing teeth, anchored by adjacent teeth or implants.

Root Canal Therapy

Root canals relieve pain and save infected teeth while preserving your natural smile.

Dentures

Full and partial dentures offer comfortable solutions for multiple missing teeth.
