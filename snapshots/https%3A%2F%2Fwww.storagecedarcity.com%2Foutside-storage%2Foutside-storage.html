Here in Southern Utah, we know how to have fun. With Sand Hollow Reservoir, Zion and Bryce Canyon National Parks, Coral Pink Sand Dunes State Park, and so many other world-class opportunities for outdoor adventure nearby, many residents of St. George and the surrounding areas enjoy getting out in the sunshine with boats, RVs, ATVs, motorcycles, and other recreational equipment.

These types of vehicles are fun but can be a hassle to store during the off-season or between uses, and they can take up a lot of space in your driveway or garage. Airtight Storage is your one-stop storage solution, with outdoor storage lots specifically designated for both the short-term and long-term storage of boats, ATVs, RVs, and other outdoor recreation equipment.

With Airtight Storage’s outdoor storage options, you can also take advantage of our facility’s state-of-the-art security system, including a barbed wire fence with key-code gate, 24-hour video surveillance, and an on-site manager at all times.
