Our Story

Caring for All That Matters to You

We provide full spectrum care, across the full spectrum of life.

We understand that mental health is the foundation of a fulfilling and resilient life. Founded in 2015, our mission has always been to make compassionate mental health care accessible to everyone. Inspired by the idea that no one should face their struggles alone, we’ve built a platform that combines the expertise of licensed professionals with cutting-edge technology.

From personalized therapy sessions to interactive wellness tools, we provide a comprehensive approach to emotional well-being. Over the years, we’ve supported thousands of individuals in overcoming life’s challenges, building resilience, and rediscovering joy. With a focus on empathy, innovation, and connection, Wellthy is here to empower you every step of the way.

Mental Health Services

A complete range of services to support mental health, including therapy, medication, and crisis help.

Individual Therapy

Personalized one-on-one sessions with licensed therapists to address your unique mental health needs.

Online Counseling

Convenient and confidential virtual therapy sessions that you can attend from the comfort of your home.

Group Therapy

Supportive group sessions where individuals can share experiences and learn from another in a safe place.

Mindfulness and Meditation

Techniques and practices to help you stay present, reduce stress, and enhance emotional regulation.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Structured therapy aimed at identifying and changing negative thought patterns to improve mental health.

Family Counseling

Counseling sessions are designed to improve communication and resolve conflicts within family units.
