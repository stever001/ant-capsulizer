Family Owned & Operated

U.S. Slide Fastener, headquartered in Franklin, Massachusetts, is your Gold Status, stocking distributor of VELCRO®touch fasteners, YKK zippers and fastening products, UNIVERSAL snaps, TAPECRAFT webbing and narrow fabrics, buckles and other plastic and metal hardware, trimmings, cords and laces, stock and custom care and size labels, ribbon, elastic, buttons and thousands of other quality fastening and trim products, most of which are made here in the United States. In business for more than 50 years, we have the experience to source the right connection for your project. If you are not sure what you need for your particular application, contact us for application assistance and samples. Family owned and operated, we take great pride in the craftsmanship of our merchandise and our unparalleled customer service and support. We serve all of North America and are centrally located between Boston, Providence, and Worcester.
