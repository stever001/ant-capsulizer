what makes us stand out

Services and Features

At Airtight Storage, we offer a variety of services ranging from outdoor storage to our storage facility. With our outdoor storage, we have the space and capacity for you to store boats, ATVs, and RVs. All our space is rented at an affordable, competitive price so that you know you’re getting the best deal in Cedar City and Enoch!

One of our main services is our storage facility. We don’t have a traditional indoor storage facility where each client rents out a small storage unit. Instead, we offer airtight and sealed shipping containers for clients to rent out. Our self storage containers come in multiple sizes, so you only pay for the size you need.

At Airtight Storage, our storage facility features security, surveillance, 24/7 access, climate-controlled units, and more! We also offer short- and long-term renting periods so that you can store your belongings for as long as you need.

AAA Mobile Storage Partnership

We also have a unique advantage that puts us above our competitors: We partner with AAA Mobile Storage. AAA Mobile Storage has outstanding customer service and great rates as well. They provide storage containers and other portable trailers and containers that you can have delivered to you. The best part? You can use our facility to store your storage container until you need to access it.
