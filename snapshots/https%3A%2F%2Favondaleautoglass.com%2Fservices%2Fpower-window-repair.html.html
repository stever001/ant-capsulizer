Our Expertise

Common Power Window Issues We Can Resolve

At Avondale Auto Glass, we understand the frustration that comes with malfunctioning power windows. Our dedicated team is equipped to tackle an array of power window problems, including but not limited to:

  • Stuck Power Windows

    If you find yourself struggling with windows that refuse to budge, our experts are here to provide swift solutions.

  • Slow Rolling Power Windows

    Don't endure the inconvenience of sluggish window movement. We efficiently address slow rolling issues to get your windows operating smoothly.

  • Off-Track Car Windows

    If your windows have derailed from their tracks, our skilled technicians realign them to ensure proper functioning.

  • Malfunctioning Power Window Switches

    Faulty switches can disrupt the operation of your windows. Our team diagnoses and repairs such issues for optimal performance.

  • Broken Power Window Buttons

    Non-responsive buttons can hinder your control over the windows. We expertly repair or replace broken buttons to restore convenience.

  • Windows That Won't Go Up or Down

    Whether your windows are stuck in the up or down position, we diagnose the root cause and provide effective solutions.
