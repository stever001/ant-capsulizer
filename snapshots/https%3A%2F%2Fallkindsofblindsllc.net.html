Services
get in touch today for a free estimate and initial consultation

All Kinds of Blinds is family owned locally based and operated. We have been servicing the metropolitan Kansas City area for 28 years. A majority of our customer base is referrals from past customers.

At All Kinds of Blinds we use top quality materials bought direct from the manufacturer such as Hunter Douglas, Timber Blind, Graber and many others.

We will locate the product that is best for your home/business based on price, color, and quality of product!

We can and will beat any big box store because you will not be paying their overhead!!!

We go by appointment, bring samples to your home and explain the entire ordering, installation process.

We provide window treatment services to residential, independent living facilities, doctor’s offices, insurance offices, tax preparation offices, home builders display homes and many other types of retail/service providers.

The majority of our business comes from providing window treatments for private homeowners with products and services.
