CANCELLATIONS

If you need to cancel your reservation for any reason, there will be a 1-night charge + tax and credit card fee. No Shows will be charged the full amount for the entirety of the stay booked. Guests who shorten their stay are liable for charges of cancelled nights.

CLEANING FEE

Any RV Site, Tent Site or Cabin not left clean will be charged a minimum $50 cleaning fee.

TENT CAMPING SITES

These wooded campsites do not have hook-ups. They are within easy walking distance of a comfort station with hot showers and toilets. Tents are not allowed to be set up in RV sites unless prior arrangements have been made through the office.

‍Campers having visitors must pay $3 daily per person and must not exceed the maximum number of people allowed per site. Day guests are to be out by 8 PM.All rates are for 2 people. $5 per night for each extra guest. Maximum 4 people (including visitors) and 1 car per tent site.

Contact park office for exceptions. No pets allowed. Tent camping reservations can only be made by calling the park office.‍

CEDAR CABIN

1 Queen bed (private), 1 Sofa Bed, 1 bunk bed (full on the bottom, twin on top), bathroom, full kitchen, dining area.

The cabin has a picnic table, a fire pit, a charcoal grill and a swing, all under nice big shade trees and near the river. Campfires are allowed if we are not in a burn ban. Pets are not allowed in the cabin, no exceptions. There is no cabin cleaning fee as long as you leave the cabin as you found it!  Rates are for 2 people. $5 per night for each extra guest. Maximum 6 people and 1 car.

BUNKHOUSE CABIN (Sleeps 4)

The bunkhouse has a queen trundle bed. There is electricity to the bunkhouse, and it has a small refrigerator, microwave, coffee pot, fan and heater. It does not have plumbing, but is located next to our restroom/showers. No cleaning fee for minimum 2 night stays.  Pets are not allowed in the bunkhouse, no exceptions.  Rates are for 2 people. $5 per night for each extra guest. Maximum 4 people and 1 car.
