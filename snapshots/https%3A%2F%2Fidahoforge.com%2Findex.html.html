Iron Railings & Iron Gates

Custom Iron Work: Iron Railings, Iron Gates, Furniture & More in Boise, Idaho

At Idaho Forge, my main focus is ornamental wrought iron work from projects such as iron railings for decks, iron gates, and iron stairways to kitchen items such as cabinet pulls and pot racks. I enjoy doing all types of furniture: end tables, coffee tables, chairs, stools, mirrors, coat racks and exterior items such as trellises, plant hangars, fountains and all sorts of yard art.

All of my wrought iron work is done by hand using the several anvils I own. I also incorporate my 25 lb Little Giant trip hammer, which is over 100 years old and a 95 lb air hammer that I hand built myself. Occasionally, I employ a helper apprentice in the shop.

Skilled Craftsmanship Refined from Years of Experience

Like many blacksmiths of my generation, I was initiated into smithing through shoeing horses, which I have done for over 30 years.  I am a Journeyman level farrier in America and am registered with the Company of Worshipful Farriers of England.

Get in touch with Vince to start your next custom wrought iron project. From iron railings for your staircase, to iron gates for your home, Vince is excited to discuss your vision and bring it to life.
