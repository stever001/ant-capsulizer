Frequently Asked Questions

How long have you been preparing tax returns?

I started the tax preparation business in 1992 and stay current with the tax law changes each year.

Can you help me to file tax returns for previous years?

Yes. I can prepare tax returns for you that you have not filed from past years.

I recently moved to Texas from Oklahoma. Can you prepare my Oklahoma state tax return?

Yes, I am experienced with filing all state tax returns to include Oklahoma.

Can you review my tax return that I prepared on Turbo Tax?

Yes. If you supply a hard copy of the tax return along with the papers used in THE preparation, I can review  your tax return and advise changes at an hourly rate.

How much do you charge to prepare taxes?

My fees are mostly based on forms prepared. However, some items are billed at an hourly rate. I will quote you a price up front and advise you along the way with any changes.

How quickly can I get my tax return prepared?

The turn around is usually between 12 and 24 hours once all necessary papers have prepared/received.

How can I get copies of my past tax returns from you?

If you lose or misplace the copy that was given to you upon completion, you can access the client portal to where you will have unlimited access to your tax returns 24/7 for your convenience.
