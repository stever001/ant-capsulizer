 We Handle It For You

Here's everything we take off your plate:

We help you streamline systems, boost collections, and stay compliant — with support that adapts to your practice.

A Dedicated Insurance Billing Specialist

You get your own dedicated team, who specializes in your software, that will partner with you and work with you each day!

HIPAA Compliance & Security

At Zeroed-In, compliance is our top priority. We never connect as an administrator to your systems, and we connect to you using a VPN (Virtual Private Network).

Expert Knowledge

Your Zeroed-In Team has worked for years in Dentistry and is very familiar with contractual laws and what it takes to keep you in compliance, and get those claims paid quickly!

Proactive Claims Management

Your team will send claims the "next day" to allow time for thorough notes. We send claims daily, research rejections, and stay closely aligned with your team.

Claim Appeals

At Zeroed-In, details matter. We’ll guide you through proper notes and processes to keep the appeals at a minimum. Your Z-in Team will work every claim to get what should be paid, actually paid.

Insurance Verification & Data Entry

Now part of our Core Services — we verify every patient’s benefits by the night before. At Zeroed-In, we don’t believe in partial verifications.

Outstanding Claim Follow-Up

Also referred to as Claims Aging - we will research aging claims every 7 days to keep your claims aging at a very low level! Remember, we only get paid if you do!

Safety & Security

We will work with you to educate you on best practices, as provided by the Inspector General, to make sure you are in compliance with insurance contracts and guard against any unwanted audits!

Local & Experienced Team

As a proud Idaho-based company, we value strong relationships and bring deep experience with HIPAA training, credentialing, and dental consulting resources.
