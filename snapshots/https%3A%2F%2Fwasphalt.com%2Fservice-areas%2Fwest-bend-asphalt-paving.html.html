A City with Vibrant Heritage

Nestled in the picturesque landscape of Wisconsin, West Bend exudes a captivating blend of history and modernity. Situated in the heart of Washington County, West Bend boasts a rich legacy that resonates with the spirit of progress. Established as a thriving industrial hub, West Bend's roots trace back to the 19th century. Named after the westward bend in the Milwaukee River, the city has evolved into a vibrant urban center while cherishing its historic charm.

Surrounded by the serene beauty of the Kettle Moraine State Forest and the rolling hills of southeastern Wisconsin, West Bend offers residents and visitors alike a unique blend of natural splendor and urban convenience. The nearby Eisenbahn State Trail provides a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, offering scenic hiking and biking opportunities through lush woodlands and picturesque landscapes.

Beyond its natural allure, West Bend thrives on a commitment to community and innovation. A thriving arts and culture scene blooms here, with a spectrum of events and attractions that cater to all ages. From art galleries and museums to local festivals and farmers markets, West Bend fosters a sense of togetherness that brings the community closer.
