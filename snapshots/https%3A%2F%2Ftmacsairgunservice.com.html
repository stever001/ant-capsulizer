For all your Airgun repair and parts needs contact TMac’s Air Gun Service LLC. We offer complete service at competitive prices.

We have a large supply of Daisy parts, new old stock, obsolete and used daisy parts for sale. We have parts for the Red Ryder, Buck Jones, Buzz Barton, Sears Brands, 25, 99, 98, 95, 96, 1894, 2001, 2002, 2003, 89, 990, 111, 94, 853, 953, 840, 880, 881, 882, 93, 92, 1911, 45, 44, 717, 722, 747, 777, 1200, 200, 300, 21, 104, 1700, 88, 179, and many more. Call for available parts for your Daisy model if not listed.

We repair most brands and models including names such as Crosman, Benjamin, Sheridan, Walther, Diana, RWS, Daisy, Gamo, Remington, Smith & Wesson to name a few, including PCP’s, Co2, pneumatics, spring and spring piston models, and many others. We have parts for Crosman, Benjamin, Sheridan, and other guns also.

If you do not see your brand or model, please contact me we cannot list them all, we cannot name them all here. We repair current model or obsolete/antique air guns even Philippine guns. We are an authorized repair center for Crosman, Daisy, Gamo, Smith and Wesson and others such as Remington, Smith & Wesson, Benjamin/Sheridan, Umarex, Logan, Colt, Beretta, Walther, and others. We buy airguns, air gun parts new or used, personal collections large or small, tools and complete air gun shops. We look forward to being of service to you.

Repairs to Smith and Wesson Models: 78G, 79G, 77A, 80G.

We also repair projector (Tranquilizer) guns: Palmer Cap Chur Tranquilizer Guns, Pneudart, and other projector or tranquilizer guns

All air gun repairs performed by TMac’s Air Gun Service are as follows: We will disassemble clean and then inspect your gun for missing, worn, or damaged parts. The gun is then resealed. After resealing we test-fire all guns to evaluate performance. Guns designed to hold air or gas are static tested 24hrs to test seals before shipping.
