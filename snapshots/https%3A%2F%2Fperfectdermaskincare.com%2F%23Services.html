Our Services.

BOTOX: Botox is a cosmetic treatment that uses a diluted form of botulinum toxin to temporarily smooth away wrinkles and fine lines. It is quick, relatively painless, and results can last for several months, making it a popular option for those looking for a refreshed appearance.

ALLERGAN FILLER PRODUCTS: Ask about the Allergan Products that we offer!

PROFHILO TREATMENT: Profhilo is a revolutionary skin rejuvenation treatment that uses hyaluronic acid to hydrate and tighten the skin, resulting in a smoother, more youthful appearance.

FRAXEL LASER: Newest invention for skin resurfacing to address hyper pigmentation.

APOLLO TRIPOLLAR: Helps with skin tightening reduce fat and build collagen.

MICRODERMABRASION: Exfoliates first layer of the skin and helps eliminate wrinkles, hyper pigmentation, acne scars, and large pores.

GLYCOLIC ACID PEEL: Used with microdermabrasion to give a deeper peel and help with larger pores. Leaves the skin fresh and clean. Used for skin conditioning treatment.

PHOTO FACIAL: Monthly machine treatment that helps build up collagen for elasticity. Great for people with rosacia and redness in their skin or hyper pigmentation. For more extreme conditions, we recommend 4 to 8 sessions.

LASER HAIR REMOVAL: Eliminates unwanted hair by burning the hair follicle. We recommend 4 to 8 sessions.

LASH EXTENSIONS: Synthetic hair by Extreme Lashes. Natural looking results.

REVITALASH®: Generates longer, thicker, fuller eyelashes. You can see results within two weeks and the product itself lasts a long time. Learn more about this amazing product at Revitalash-USA.com.
