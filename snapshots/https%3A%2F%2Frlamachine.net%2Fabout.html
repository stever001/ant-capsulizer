about us

You can expect quality customer service from our experienced staff every time.

We're proud to be able to help you with all of your machine and tool needs, from sales to repairs.

We can help you with all your DRO and CNC control requirements for your Mills, Lathes, Grinders, and other Machine Tool applications. Not only do we sell the Digital Readouts and CNC Retrofit packages, we also provide installation and training of all the BRANDS we sell.
