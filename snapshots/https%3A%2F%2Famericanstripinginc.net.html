Our Services

serving sarasota & surrounding areas nationwide

Discover unparalleled expertise and dedication to airfield maintenance with American Striping Inc., your trusted partner for superior rubber and paint removal services in Sarasota, Florida. Explore our comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the specific needs for airfield maintenance.

Complete Layout Work
Our skilled technicians provide meticulous layout work, ensuring precise alignment and accurate markings on airfield runways, taxiways, and aprons. Our commitment to detail guarantees that every aspect of your runway layout meets ETL standards for safety and compliance, by the use of predetermined benchmarks and satellite coordinates.

Pre-Painting Surface Preparation
‍Before applying new paint, our expert team conducts thorough pre-painting surface preparation. This involves cleaning the surface, ensuring optimal adhesion and longevity of the paint. Our dedication to preparation results in clear and durable runway markings.

Painting
Enhance runway visibility and safety with our specialized painting service. We meticulously apply high-quality paint and beads according to specs, creating crisp, well-defined markings that guide pilots and ground crews with precision, even under varying weather conditions.

Rubber/Paint Removal
Our advanced rubber/paint removal service restores the integrity of your runway surface. Using cutting-edge equipment and techniques, we efficiently remove rubber/paint buildup contributing to improved coefficient of friction and enhanced runway performance.

Runway Cleaning
‍Thorough and efficient cleaning services to maintain the integrity of your airfield's runway surface.

Paint Removal Services
‍Professional paint removal solutions to ensure clear and visible markings on runways, taxiways, and aprons.

Customized Solutions:
Tailored solutions for rubber and paint removal, addressing specific requirements of each project.

Re-Painting Services
High-quality re-painting of runways to enhance visibility safety conditions and ensure compliance with aviation regulations/ETL.
