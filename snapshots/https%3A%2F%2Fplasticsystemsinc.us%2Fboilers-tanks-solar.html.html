Instant Hot Water Heaters, Boilers, & More

Plastic Systems, Inc. offers much more than reliable indoor air quality systems.

Group Thermo
Ergomax heat exchangers feature 99% heat transfer, are square for smaller install footprints, and require no recovery. They are double wall exempt, and have the industry’s best commercial warranty. Six models from 121 To 1,800 gph/unit, up To 190 degrees Fahrenheit.

HBX Control Systems, Inc.
CPU based programmable controls for boilers & chillers. Used for staging, mixing, DHW, and dual systems. Compatible with modulating boilers and geo systems, these feature data plotting, alarm status, and troubleshooting. The modular design allows for up to 6 expansion modules.

Monitor Tankless Gas Water Heater
180,000 BTU INSTANT tankless hot water heater. Available in natural gas and propane models; direct vent, indoor and outdoor models. Features direct electronic ignition, digital remote controller, a quick response mode for rapid heating with a modulating burner, and up to 5.8 GPM.

Tamas Hydronics
Hydronic control panels for primary and secondary systems. Featuring low and high temp zone panels, combination, and injection zone panels; and
pre-piped, pre-wired printed circuit boards.

Watts Radiant
Floor heating, snow melting, and domestic PEX. A full line of hydronic radiant accessories & manifolds, ONIX radiant tubing and radiant PEX tubing as well as SubRay radiant floor system, HeatWeave and WarmWire electric radiant floor.

Peerless Boilers
Peerless boilers are the practical, efficient, clean alternative to furnaces and heat pumps.
