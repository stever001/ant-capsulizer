Just Another Day in Paradise!

Owners Donna, Matt & Melissa Bell of Along the River RV Park are glad to welcome you to Ruidoso, New Mexico! Whether you are a first-time camper with us or have been here before, Along the River has a campsite for you!

Along the River RV Park has been nominated by the Ruidoso Valley Chamber of Commerce for "Member of the Year" in 2016. and voted "Best RV Park in Lincoln County" for 2016!

NEARBY ATTRACTIONS

Some of the nearby attractions include Ski Apache, the Smokey Bear Historical Park & Museum in Capitan and hiking in the Lincoln National Forest.

Also close by is the Valley of Fires Recreation Area near Carrizozo, Three Rivers Petroglyph Site and wineries near Tularosa, the Lodge Resort at Cloudcroft, Fort Stanton, and the town of Lincoln, which is famous for the Lincoln County War and is the historical home of Billy the Kid.

The White Sands National Monument and the New Mexico Museum of Space History are in nearby Alamogordo. And, we can't forget Ruidoso which has something for everyone whether it be restaurants, casinos, horse racing (when in season), golf, beautiful drives,shopping, and concerts.

After enjoying some of the nearby attractions of this beautiful area, you may want to just relax in the park enjoying awesome mountain views, wildlife, and star studded nights by your campfire away from the hustle and bustle of town.

We offer more than a place to stay, we present a delightful and memorable experience. Our RV Park is for every age group, so give us a call and come see us soon!

ACCESSIBLITY & ACCOMMODATIONS

  • Paved Highway frontage
  • On the Rio Bonito River
  • Cabins & Tenting
  • Minutes from many activities
  • Campfires allowed (if not prohibited by burn ban)
  • River sites & Non-river sites
  • Big Rig Friendly
  • Big, wide & long pull-thrus
  • 50-30-20 amp with full hookups
  • WiFi
  • Pets allowed in RV's (Limit 1 with no extra cost). More than 1 are $5 each per day, maximum of 3 dogs. Pets are not allowed in pop-ups, hybrids, tents or cabins.
