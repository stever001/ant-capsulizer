Welcome to JM Products Inc.

Supply your company with professionally machined parts and products from our experts in Oxford, Connecticut.

J.M. Products Inc. is located in Oxford, Connecticut right near the Oxford Airport just one mile away from Exit 16 off of I 84. Our facility is fully equipped with new CNC lathes and milling machines ready to handle your manufacturing requirements. We are a family owned and operated machine shop. We have been in business for over a half-century. The key to our longevity is our unwavering attention to customer service. Our team is always available to answer your questions or help you solve a problem. We have the experience to solve even your most complex manufacturing problems. In addition to our service, our customers really appreciate our short lead times and competitive pricing. Our average lead-time is tow to three weeks. We understand our customers’ need to keep inventory and other costs down in this uncertain economy.
