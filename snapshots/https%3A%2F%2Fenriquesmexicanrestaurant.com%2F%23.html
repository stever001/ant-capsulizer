About Us

founded in 1983

Family owned and operated we are proud to serve the best authentic Mexican food for over 30 years. Enrique along with his wife and sister-in-law started in 1983 doing it all along with a couple other family members helping out. Enrique and his daughter Mercedes are now proud of the 35 employee team and proud to serve customers from all over the world. Beginning with only 8 tables and a very small menu we have expanded over 5 times and now offer Mexican, American and Cuban dishes on menu. Located inside the airport not only is the food unique but so is the view. Enjoy the planes landing and taking off as you enjoy the best food in town. We strive to welcome our guests like family and are dedicated to giving you a memorable experience every time. We have a great team filled with experience and training to offer you the best from start to finish.

About Us

A LITTLE HISTORY

Welcome to Corona Village Restaurant; an authentic “Taste of Mexico”. Corona Village came into being over 25 years ago in the sleepy southwestern Idaho town of Meridian. Founded on the idea of offering original Mexican cuisine served in a relaxing atmosphere. Today the company includes restaurants in several states throughout the western United States; Since the beginning entrees have been prepared using only the freshest produce and finest cuts of meat. From our homemade chips and salsa to our hand prepared dishes with originality found only at a Corona Village Restaurant.

Corona Village provides you with a unique dining experience by offering traditional Mexican fare that is the best-tasting in the Treasure Valley. Come in and dine with us to enjoy a real taste of Mexico!

FRESHNESS YOU CAN TASTE

At Corona Village Mexican Restaurants all meals are freshly made each day with high quality ingredients to ensure superior flavor. Your meal will be cooked-to-order so that you get exactly what you want.

You’ll never leave feeling hungry with the oversized meals that are served. With  the reasonable prices we offer, you can afford to treat the whole family.

Popular Dishes

Delicious Menu Favorites

Browse some of our most popular house specialties recommended by Chef Modesto.

Nachos Supreme

Our homemade chips smothered with cheddar & jack cheese, beans, tomatoes, chives, jalapeno, sour cream, guacamole and topped with your choice of beef, chicken or pork to finish this fiesta platter.

Fajitas

Our sizzling fajitas are grilled to order. Your choice of steak, chicken, pork or shrimp grilled with onion and Anaheim peppers, Rice, ranchero beans, sour cream, pico de gallo, cheese and tortillas served on the side. Add a delicious vegetable medley for an additional charge.

Combination Platter

Three of our popular menu items: a beef enchilada, a bean tostada and a crispy beef taco served with rice and ranchero beans

Tampico Fiesta

One of our Cuban specialties; an 8oz ribeye OR 2 pork chops topped with grilled onion, colored bell peppers and shrimp; served with white rice, black beans and our plantains on the side.

Texas Hot & Spicy Burrito

One of our original dishes; this burrito is sure to set your mouth on fire! Stuffed and topped with chuck roast, whole beans and a spicy jalapeno gravy; topped off with melted jack cheese and chives; your choice of a small burrito served with rice and guacamole OR one large burrito.

Shrimp Ranchero

Cuban inspired dish; shrimp sautéed in our creamy green chili sauce with pico de gallo. Served with white rice, black beans and a fresh salad topped with colored bell peppers.

Taco Salad

Crispy flour shell filled with refried beans, lettuce, tomato, chives, cheese, guacamole and your choice of beef, chicken or pork.

Chili Relleno

Stuffed poblano pepper filled with your choice of beef or cheese then dipped in batter and fried to perfection, topped with our delicious ranchero sauce or queso.
