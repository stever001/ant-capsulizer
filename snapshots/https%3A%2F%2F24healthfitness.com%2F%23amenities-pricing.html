Health & Fitness In The Heights is a locally owned and operated, 24 hour fitness center in Little Rock, Arkansas. We offer professional fitness services, personal training, a proven weight loss program and our group classes are all included with your membership.

If you are looking for a clean comfortable place to get in shape and feel great about yourself, then look no further than Health & Fitness In The Heights. It’s our mission to help you reach your fitness goals. Stop by and pick up a free pass or you can download it below, all you need is a local ID.

our services

It is our goal to make your workout facility the best in Little Rock. Our full line of professional state-of-the-art cardio and strength equipment was designed to fit all the needs of our members. Our courteous front desk staff will greet you and check you in during normal business hours. After hours, your access card will allow you into the facility any time of the day. A surveillance system offers security for your safety, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Be sure to stop in and we’ll gladly assist you with all your needs and answer any questions.
