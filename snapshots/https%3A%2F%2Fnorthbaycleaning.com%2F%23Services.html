About Us

Professional cleaning and maintenance services since 1993.

Over the years we have developed a great amount of experience working in commercial and industrial buildings, performing complete janitorial services ranging from carpet care to pressure washing and more. We have an expert cleaning crew that strives to continually exceed our customers’ expectations and ensure service excellence.

At National Building Maintenance, Inc. we understand the importance of time scheduling and the need for cleaning activities to be performed around the normal daily operating schedule. For this reason we begin our client relationships with a thorough evaluation of the facility. This allows us to develop a customized plan of action and to properly schedule our visits accordingly.

Allow us the opportunity to evaluate your place of business to see how we can help lower your cleaning costs while improving its cleanliness. At National Building Maintenance, Inc. our clients’ needs are our priority because our clients always come first.

Proudly Serving: The Greater Bay Area – Including the East Bay (Oakland and surrounding cities) The South Bay (San Jose and surrounding cities) The Peninsula (Burlingame, San Mateo, and surrounding cities), The City and County of San Francisco, and the North Bay Area - Including Santa Rosa, Rohnert Park, San Rafael, Petaluma, and Novato.
