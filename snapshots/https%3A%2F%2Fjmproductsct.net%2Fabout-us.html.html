About Us

Supply your company with professionally machined parts and products from our experts in Oxford, Connecticut.

Founded in 1965, J.M. Products, Inc. is a dynamic company that manufactures and supplies CNC machined parts. We have been providing precision parts to a wide variety of industries for the past 45 years. We still supply our original customer, which is a testament to our service and quality.

Why do our customers keep coming back?

We use State of the Art equipment and software to produce precision parts with the shortest lead times possible.

Our CNC lathes and milling machines deliver the precision our customers require. We utilize Computer Aided Design (CAD/CAM) software to increase our operating efficiencies, thereby lowering costs to our customers while improving quality. The competitive market place today demands reduced lead times and date-critical delivery. J.M. Products has the commitment, capacity and abilities to provide precision components in a timely manner and meet delivery schedules. We work with customers who have various inventory systems such as Kanban and Just-in-Time (JIT). We also work with customer-approved vendors at our customers direction.

We have an experienced, quality-driven workforce trained to meet customers needs.

Continuous training is essential to ensuring that our machinists are up to date with the latest techniques in machining. At J.M. Products, we continuously invest in new technology and equipment to enhance our precision making capabilities and reduce our lead times. To achieve these goals, our machinists must frequently undergo in-depth, hands on training by professionals in the field. Our experienced machinists adapt quickly to new technology, which ensures a smooth transition. We value our employees and the experience that they bring to every part they work on.

We strive to continuously improve all aspects of our operations so that we may serve our customers better. To help us achieve this goal we are ISO 9001:2000 registered.

At JM Products we understand that our success is dependent upon our customers success. Continuous improvement is key to staying competitive. To stay on top, we keep up with the latest advancements in technology that is why over 80% of our CNC machines are less than two years old. We have the experience and know how to give shorter lead times than most of our competitors and we strive to continue to make them even shorter. We are there for our customers when they need us. Our knowledgeable staff is always available to answer questions and work with customers to solve their problems. Whether it is a part that they need manufactured right away or a quick design change, we are here to help.

We would welcome the opportunity to discuss with you how we can help your company meet its strategic goals. Please feel free to call us at (203) 267-6434.
