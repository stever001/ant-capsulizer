Welcome to Carolina Custom Window Treatments

WINDOW TREATMENTS, SHADES, BLINDS, CUSTOM DESIGN & MORE!

Carolina Custom Window Treatments is here to help you discover the perfect decor for all the windows in your home.

If you are looking for custom designed window treatments for your living room, or desire to have someone come in install custom drapery or blinds for your entire home; then come to Carolina Custom Window Treatments, LLC based in San Diego, California. Our friendly staff of designers offers the best in customized window treatments. We have a variety of products to choose from including drapery, panels, valances, shades, swags, and so much more. You are sure to find the perfect fabric, color and texture for any room of your home. Carolina Custom Window Treatments also offers quick shipping services. One of our knowledgeable professionals will come to your home and provide you with a free consultation and estimate for all the window treatments you desire.
