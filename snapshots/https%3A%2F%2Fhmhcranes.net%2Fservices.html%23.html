Inspection Services

Keeping Equipment Safe and Dependable

Ensure your equipment is OSHA compliant with our inspection services. HMH Industries crew of experienced personnel and quality technicians are all prepared to help you with any services and inspections your job site requires. We specialize in performing services and inspections for AC, DC, and air cranes (overhead & gantry) as well as jib and monorail cranes. Contact us at (888) 482-4943 to see how we can help you stay safe when using your overhead cranes and maintain compliance.

Structural Inspection Services
Trust us to provide you with a very thorough inspection of your entire lifting structure. When making a lift with an overhead crane everything is a factor from the building footing, building columns, runway, and structure of the crane all play part in your lifting system. We’ll provide you with a meticulous report of the condition of your runway crane structure and inform you of any problems we find during the inspection.

Electrical Inspection Services
Make sure your cranes are operating properly and safely with our inspection services. The electrical systems on your crane are essential to proper and safe operation. We inspect all electrical enclosures, limit switches, safety devices, energy isolation devices, and power distribution systems including conductor and festoon systems. With this inspection you will also get recommendations on repairs or up grades that will save you money and down time.

Mechanical Inspection Services
Mechanical component problems can be identified often before a breakdown occurs. Identifying these problems in mechanical systems before a brake down will save money and reduce down time. Our inspection includes a complete visual inspection of the conditions of the mechanical components on your crane including all gearboxes, bearings, line shafts, coupling, and gearing. We also offer fiber optic inspection of gearboxes when needed.
