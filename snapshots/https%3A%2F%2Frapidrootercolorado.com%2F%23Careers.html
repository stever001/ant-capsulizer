Our Promise To You

Trying to choose the right home service company can be confusing and frustrating. Who can you trust for expert skills, honest pricing, and clean work performed by courteous technicians? For nearly 5 decades, that kind of professionalism has been the Rapid Rooter family way. At Rapid Rooter of Colorado we are plumbing contractors specializing in water heater repairs, and sewer line replacements.

That means we service your home with the kind of care and commitment that only family pride can deliver, and you’ll find our service is as friendly as Front Range Colorado itself. After all, we grew up here hiking, biking, dining, & participating in our schools, parks & community programs. Our references go back decades.
