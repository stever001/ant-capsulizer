The Bridal Chateau carefully selects every gown we offer in the store. Just a little background information on a few of the bridal designer lines we carry . . .

Casablanca Bridal

Lace, femininity, and delicate, rich details are what you see from Casablanca. Casablanca gowns are unsurpassed in quality and workmanship.

DaVinci Bridal

DaVinci Bridal offers stunning styles and quality at prices that brides find very affordable.

Demetrios Bridal

Elegant and contemporary styles featuring sparkle, beading, and dramatic lines.

Gown Preservation

The Bridal Chateau is an Official Retailer of Wedding Gown Preservation Company

They have been cleaning and preserving bridal gowns since 1913. They provide expert cleaning and preserving along with a certified guarantee. Their website provides all the information you need to begin the process. You are also welcome to call The Bridal Chateau if you have any questions.
