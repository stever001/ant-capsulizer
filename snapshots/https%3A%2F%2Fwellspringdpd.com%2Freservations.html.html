The Bishops' House

A UNIQUE PART OF BOISE'S HERITAGE

The Bishops' House has become a desired destination is Boise as a venue host for big events. A grand, sweeping veranda wraps around three sides of the house, with entry to the Foyer, Front Parlor, Dining Room, Music Room and Service Kitchen. Up the open stairway, the second floor features the Bride's and Groom's Rooms.

The Bride's Room, within the curve of the turret, features floral wallpaper, lace curtains, and a chandelier; and it opens onto a small balcony.

The Groom's Room sports elegant, richly colored striped wallpaper and carpeting. Period chairs provide seating, while paintings and drawings accent the walls.

The Garden at The Bishops' House includes a Gazebo, Arch from the Rose Garden, and Shade Garden along the picturesque rock wall. Additionally, the front wall exhibits traditional flower paintings, trees and shrubs, perfect for photography and providing space for relaxing and enjoying the ambience of this unique setting.
