Design Engineering & New Product Development

Let us help you realize your dream product

Grow your business and meet your technological needs with product development from Wellspring Design & Product Development. Based in Blythewood, South Carolina, we build products like self-service automation kiosks and custom computers to better suit your business’ needs. By testing your product before it ships, we can guarantee that your product will operate at top productivity levels. We also design and test packaging to ensure your product arrives safely.

Building & Testing

We design and test shipping packaging to help ensure that merchandise isn’t damaged during transport. Full set product integrity and mechanical testing includes vibrating the product to see how well it handles shock.

learn more

Product Design

Our services offer ways for people with good ideas to see their product realized mechanically. We have a full suite of mechanical CAD design software that we will use to create a 3D design of your invention. From the 3D model, changes are easily made before the product goes into production.

Product Improvement

In addition to working with new products, Wellspring Design & Product Development works with existing products to look for improvements and quality through analysis and testing.
