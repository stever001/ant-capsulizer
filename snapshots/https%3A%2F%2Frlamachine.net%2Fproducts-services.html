We focus on the ACU-RITE Solutions Digital Readout systems and repair parts for manual Mills, Lathes, Grinders, and all other applications for DRO’s. We are able to service and supply parts for other brands of DRO’s as well.

At present our focus for CNC controls is the ACU-RITE Solutions MILLPWR control. TRADE-IN programs are available for your old tried or non-functioning DRO’s and CNC controls. Our Sales, Technical Support and Service staff have many years of experience with most of the DRO’s and Controls sold throughout the years.

If you have older models of the ACU-RITE Solutions MILLPWR control, RLA Machine & Tool Sales, Inc. offers UPGRADE packages to the latest models. Please call us so we can discuss your needs.

TRADE-IN programs are available for your old tried or non-functioning DRO’s and CNC controls, regardless of the brand, call us about our trade in programs. We also provide trade in programs for older CNC controls.

RLA Machine & Tool Sales, Inc. has the capability of doing some repair and rebuilding in house, but work closely with some major rebuilding houses. So when you find the need to have some machine maintenance done, call us to discuss your requirements, whether it is just repair parts or actual repair services.

If you are in the market for Tooling & Accessories for your mills, lathes and grinders, we handle MACH-1 quick change tooling for both your manual and CNC Mills. Viseman is a vise handling & storage device to help prevent the wear and tear on you vise, mill and your machine operators. If you need a power feed for your mills X or Y axis table or the more important Knee power feed on your CNC mills, we have them.

Maybe you need low cost CAD/CAM design software for the new CNC machine you just purchased or you just need a CAD /CAM package on the shop floor for the quick and easy projects your operators are working.  Call us, we offer BobCad/Cam design software.
