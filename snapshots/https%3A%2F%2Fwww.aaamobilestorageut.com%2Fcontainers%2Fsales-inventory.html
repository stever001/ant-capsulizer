Storage Containers

Storage containers, or shipping containers, are made of extra-durable steel and meant for international cargo shipping. Thus, these containers are airtight—excellent at withstanding the elements and providing safe and secure storage. They also keep rodents and other pests out and require minimal upkeep compared to traditionally built storage structures.

Renting vs. Buying

Storage can be a difficult thing to manage. This is true for many different types of industry, business, and even residential uses. Many individuals find themselves wondering if they should rent or buy their storage unit. Although some people prefer to always rent because of the smaller amount due upfront, it’s generally wise to evaluate the price of each option and opt for the route that has the lowest cost over the long run.

Often, people rent containers expecting to only use them for a short few months, but life gets busy, and they find themselves still paying rent a year later. Try to be realistic when planning your use and give yourself some cushion time. Remember, renting still incurs delivery and other upfront fees. When you purchase your container outright, you have the possibility of selling it used later to recoup some of the purchase cost.

Instead of renting space in a storage unit at monthly rates, why not consider the use of your own storage container? Brought right to you on your land or project area, the mobile storage containers in our inventory are ready to serve no matter where you are located.
