OUr Services

We would like to take this opportunity to introduce ourselves and familiarize you with our hydraulic field service company, Certified Hydraulics.

Our technicians have their journeyman cards for electro-hydraulic machinery mechanics and are registered with the Department of Labor. They also have Industrial Hydraulic Technician certifications with the Fluid Power Association. We have many years of experience with fluid power applications.

Certified Hydraulics has been in business since 1998. We have extensive experience with all types of hydraulic systems including industrial hydraulics (sawmill, papermill, window manufacturing, presses, HP water cutting, etc.), and mobile equipment (pile drivers, forklifts, sideloaders, Zamboni, etc.).
