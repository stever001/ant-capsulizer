Welcome to San Luis Obispo County!

Surf Cab Co / Wave Haul proudly serves the California Coats with reliable, efficient, and timely taxicab services. Our affordable prices make it easy for you to get to your destination, whether you need a ride home from the airport, or you need a ride to one of our many amazing local attractions. Our company services the northern two regions of the county and the San Luis Obispo County Airport with smooth riding from our comfortable Crown Victoria cabs.

Whether for tourists or locals, Surf Cab Co / Wave Haul will provide prompt cab service for an immediate pickup or for a reservation made up to three weeks in advance. We’ll deliver you anywhere you need to go, whether it’s to the grocery store or to San Francisco! Our clients love or transportation service to and from the many local wineries, summer camps, historical places, and beaches.

delivery services

In addition to our quality transportation services, Surf Cab Co / Wave Haul will do your shopping and deliver it right to your front door. From groceries and dry cleaning to restaurants and clothing stores, if it fits in the cab, we’ll pick it up! We even go through drive-thrus! Restaurants we’ve picked up from in the past include McDonald’s, In-n-Out Burger, Taco Bell, The Great American Fish Company, Schooner’s Wharf, and McClintock’s. When you need something at home but haven’t the time or energy to grab it yourself, call Surf Cab Co / Wave Haul to give you a hand.
