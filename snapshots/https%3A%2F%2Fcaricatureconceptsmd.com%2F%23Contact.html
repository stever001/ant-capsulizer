Welcome to Caricature Concepts

enliven your next party or event!

With over 30 year's experience, Doug can tailor the caricatures to your event: black and white or color, front view or profile, just faces or with little bodies incorporating the subject's favorite hobby or sport. Always a big hit! Doug draws amazing likenesses in a matter of minutes. He specializes in quick caricatures, which are humorous cartoon portraits. He also draws caricatures from photos, which make great gifts.

Doug started drawing caricatures with his dad, Irv Finifter, at the Face Place, in Ocean City, Maryland, and later at Harborplace in Baltimore. In 1983, Doug opened his own stand at the Old Post Office Pavilion in Washington, D.C.
